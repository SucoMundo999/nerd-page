Nerd Page


Iron Lung

"Iron Lung" is an intense indie adventure game that immerses you in the quest for mysterious artifacts beneath the crimson waves of a desolate moon's blood ocean. Piloting a weathered and intricately challenging submarine, you'll navigate the treacherous depths in search of these enigmatic relics.

Liminal Gallery

"Liminal Gallery" stands out as a distinctive game, pushing players to unravel intricate puzzles by reshaping reality through the utilization of photographs and a camera. It introduces an entirely novel perspective, employing optical illusions as a captivating gameplay style.

Starfield

"Starfield," Bethesda's highly anticipated title set to launch in 2023, promises an ambitious journey into a sprawling and immersive space universe. Players will pilot their own spacecraft, engaging in intense first and third-person combat, and experiencing a remarkable ship navigation system that offers near-limitless exploration opportunities.
